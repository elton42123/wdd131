Uganda, the Pearl of Africa, is a biodiversity hotspot located on the coast of East Africa. Known as the "Pearl of Africa" due to its unique wildlife, climate, soils, hospitality of the people, and so much more. Uganda is home to species found nowhere else on Earth, including crested cranes, kobs, and over 1,000 species of wildlife.

The country's diverse landscapes range from equatorial rainforests to tropic climatic conditions, making it a fascinating place with rich cultural heritage and natural wonders that continue to amaze researchers and visitors alike.
